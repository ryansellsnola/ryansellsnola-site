What most FSBO sellers find out too late

Selling on your own is legal — and sometimes it works. But most sellers go in without understanding what buyers and their agents are actually doing. This toolkit gives you the full picture.

Bargain hunters target FSBOs

Buyers know you're trying to save the commission. Many approach private sellers expecting a discount equal to exactly what you saved.

Legal exposure is real

Disclosure requirements in Louisiana are strict. Missing one can open you up to lawsuits long after closing.

The net gain math surprises people

After concessions, carrying costs, and the time on market, many FSBO sellers net less than they would have with an agent.
