About This Home

Located in the heart of the French Quarter, this 2-bedroom, 1-bath condo at 940 Orleans Avenue, Unit 6 offers 627 square feet of classic New Orleans architecture within a historic main house built in 1835. The residence opens to a traditional brick courtyard that reflects the character of the Quarter.

Brick flooring runs throughout the unit alongside exposed masonry walls. The primary bedroom is equipped with blackout curtains and double-pane windows, cutting 25 to 30dB of sound from nearby street activity along Burgundy Street. French doors connect the living space to the shared courtyard for natural light and a real indoor-outdoor connection.

Two blocks from Bourbon Street and close to Royal Street's galleries, dining, and cultural offerings, this is a rare chance to own a well-preserved piece of French Quarter history in a highly sought-after location.

  • Two bedrooms at this price point in the Quarter is rare
  • Gas furnace, more efficient than electric heat in winter

  • In-unit laundry, granite countertops, stainless appliances
